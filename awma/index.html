About the association

The African Muslim Womens Association empowers Muslim women through mentorship, professional development, cross-border learning, and cultural celebration. We champion dignity, family stability, and active leadership while honoring Islamic values and African heritage.

Our Objectives

  1. Provide faith-based mentorship and spiritual enrichment for Muslim women.

  2. Create business, investment, entrepreneurship, and partnership platforms to empower women economically.

  3. Facilitate seminars, conferences, and outdoor forums that promote leadership and knowledge exchange.

  4. Strengthen family and marriage stability through structured programs and community engagement.

  5. Promote modest fashion, dignity, and cultural identity in line with Islamic and African values.

  6. Increase representation of Muslim women in leadership and decision-making spaces.

  7. Build continental solidarity among Muslim women through unity, cooperation, and advocacy.

  8. Support women affected by cancer and obstetric fistula with education, advocacy, and healthcare partnerships.
